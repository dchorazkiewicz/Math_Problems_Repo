## Task 8

### Given

Total pulses: 7

4 pulses of type A  
2 pulses of type B  
1 pulse of type C

### 1. First pulse is A

Probability equals the proportion of A pulses.

P = 4 / 7

### 2. First pulse is A or C

Possible pulses: A or C

P = (4 + 1) / 7

P = 5 / 7

### 3. First two pulses are A then C

First choose A:

P = 4 / 7

Then choose C from remaining pulses:

P = 1 / 6

Multiply:

P = (4/7)(1/6)

P = 4 / 42

P = 2 / 21
