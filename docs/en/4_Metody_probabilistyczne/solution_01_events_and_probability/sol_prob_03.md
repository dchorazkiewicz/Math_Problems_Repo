## Task 3

Possible times: 4, 5, 6 hours  
Possible errors: 0, 1, 2

Each pair (time, errors) is an elementary event.

Total number of outcomes:

3 × 3 = 9

### 1. Job completed in 4 hours

Possible outcomes:

(4,0), (4,1), (4,2)

P = 3 / 9 = 1 / 3

### 2. Job completed flawlessly in 6 hours

Flawless means 0 errors.

Outcome:

(6,0)

P = 1 / 9

### 3. Job completed in at most 5 hours

Times 4 or 5.

Number of outcomes: 6

P = 6 / 9 = 2 / 3

### 4. At most 5 hours and at most one error

Outcomes:

(4,0), (4,1), (5,0), (5,1)

P = 4 / 9
