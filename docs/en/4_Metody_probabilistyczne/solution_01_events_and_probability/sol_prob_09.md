## Task 9

### Given

Three plants produce goods.

Probability of first quality:

Plant 1: 0.97  
Plant 2: 0.90  
Plant 3: 0.86

One product is taken from each plant.

### Solution

The event that all products are first quality means all three independent events occur.

Therefore we multiply probabilities:

P = 0.97 × 0.90 × 0.86

P = 0.751
