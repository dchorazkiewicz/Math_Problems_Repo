## Problem statement

Solve

$$
XA=B
$$

for $X$, where

$$
A=\begin{pmatrix}1&1\\0&2\end{pmatrix},
\qquad
B=\begin{pmatrix}3&1\\4&2\end{pmatrix}.
$$

Pay attention to the side on which $A^{-1}$ must be multiplied.
