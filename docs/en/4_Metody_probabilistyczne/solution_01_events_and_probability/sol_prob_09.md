## Task 9

### Given

Three plants produce goods.

Probability of first quality:

Plant 1: 0.97  
Plant 2: 0.90  
Plant 3: 0.86

One product is taken from each plant.

### Idea

The products are taken from different plants, so the events are **independent**.

The probability that **all products are first quality** is the product of the individual probabilities.

### Solution

P = 0.97 × 0.90 × 0.86

P = 0.8748 × 0.86

P = 0.752328

### Result

P ≈ 0.752
