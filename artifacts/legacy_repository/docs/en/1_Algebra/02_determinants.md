## Determinants

### Ex 1. 

Calculate the determinant of the matrix

$$
A=\begin{pmatrix}
2 & 3 & 1\\
0 & -1 & 4\\
5 & 2 & 0
\end{pmatrix}
\quad
B=\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 2\\
4 & 0 & 0\\
7 & 8 & 9
\end{pmatrix}
\qquad
C=\begin{pmatrix}
3 & 0 & 2\\
2 & 0 & -2\\
0 & 1 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
$$

using Sarrus' rule.

### Ex 9. 

Show that the equality holds

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 1 & 1 \\
x & y & z \\
x^2 & y^2 & z^2
\end{vmatrix}
= (z-x)(z-y)(y-x)
$$

Prove a similar equality for the determinant

$$
\begin{vmatrix}
1 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\
x & y & z & u \\
x^2 & y^2 & z^2 & u^2 \\
x^3 & y^3 & z^3 & u^3
\end{vmatrix}
$$
