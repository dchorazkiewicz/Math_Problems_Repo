## Task 5

### Given

P(A) = 0.6  
P(B) = 0.7  
P(A ∪ B) = 0.8

### 1. Complement of A

P(A') = 1 − P(A)

P(A') = 1 − 0.6 = 0.4

### 2. Intersection of A and B

Using the formula

P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B)

0.8 = 0.6 + 0.7 − P(A ∩ B)

0.8 = 1.3 − P(A ∩ B)

P(A ∩ B) = 0.5

### 3. Probability that the water volume is not in either interval

P(A' ∩ B')

This is the complement of the union:

P(A' ∩ B') = 1 − P(A ∪ B)

P(A' ∩ B') = 1 − 0.8 = 0.2
