# Start Here with AI

Download or share the single onboarding document:

[Open `START_HERE_WITH_AI.md`](https://github.com/dchorazkiewicz/Math_Problems_Repo/blob/main/START_HERE_WITH_AI.md)

It explains the purpose of the course, the roles of lectures and exercise classes, what to do after a lecture, where the PDF and problem sources are, how notes and AI should be used, what is assessed, and where to find detailed instructions.

You can upload that Markdown file to an AI chat and ask:

> Read this course-orientation document and use its links when I ask for details. Explain what the lectures and exercise classes require, where the materials are, and how I should prepare my work. Do not guess the current teaching week or timetable.
